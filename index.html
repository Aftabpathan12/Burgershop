Our Desi Story

Serving desi happiness since 2015

From Humble Beginnings to Desi Burger Royalty

Burger Palace started as a small food truck in Mumbai with a big dream: to create the perfect desi burger. What began as a family passion project has grown into a beloved local institution across India.
